Giving for Pets provides meals for all types of pets. We will accept unopened bags or cans of animal foods to include dog, cat, rodent, bird, reptile and fish foods. Additionally, we also accept food and water containers. Instead of throwing away old tupperware, donate them to Giving for Pets and it can help provide a meal or drink for an animal!
